FULL-SERVICE
WEDDINGS

From the first tasting to the final toast, we take care of every detail — from custom menus and seamless planning to a beautifully rustic venue that sets the perfect tone for your perfect day.

Set along the picturesque Casselman River, our full-service weddings offer everything for a seamless, unforgettable day. Exchange vows in a stunning riverfront ceremony, enhanced by custom florals and a beautifully curated reception. Enjoy a buffet or plated dinner with customizable options, followed by a sparkling toast and a wedding cake designed to your vision. From planning to execution, our expert team handles every detail, allowing you to savor each moment. For intimate gatherings of 25 or less, we create an elegant, personalized experience, ensuring your wedding is filled with joy, beauty, and lasting memories.

In order to get your date on the calendar, we require a 50% deposit of your event total. One month prior to your event, the remaining balance is due. Deposits are non-refundable.
