OUR
HISTORY

The story of The Mountain Grape and Maple and Vine is one of family, heritage, and community—woven together by generations of hard work, dedication, and a love for great food.

Paul, a fourth-generation Grantsville businessman and lifelong public servant, grew up surrounded by the hum of his family's enterprises. His ancestors ran Grantsville's hotel and tavern for 25 years, while his grandparents and great-grandparents built and ran the Grantsville Lodge & Grange in the late 1930s. But perhaps most influential of all was his grandmother Sheila—born of Italian immigration into Arizona—whose legendary meatball recipe left a lasting imprint on generations of local kids. Her passion for food became Paul's, shaping many into the flavors of his heritage at the heart of Appalachia.

Emily, a fourth-generation businesswoman, was born and raised in nearby Accident, Maryland. She spent her earliest childhood—just 1.5 years old—helping her mother in the family garden. A driven Maple kid, she grew up immersed in the traditions of her hometown, instilled with a love for the land, and the rich heritage of her father (now Mayor of Grantsville). With a great-writing career in journalism, widely appreciated, and a history of public service and leadership, Emily has consistently told the story of her community as a singer of her passion for service, food, and history.

Together, Paul and Emily have created The Mountain Grape, a place where Appalachian warmth meets the best flavors of its day. You're not just finding food, you're experiencing the fruits of a legacy, and the welcoming embrace of a family that has opened its arms to every visitor.

For them, this isn't just a business—it's a love letter to their roots, their families, and the land that shaped them. Whether you're here for a big celebration, a homemade meal, a glass of wine, or a taste of something truly special, their story welcomes you with open arms—just as their families have for generations.

MEET CHEF
MARTIN GALLOWAY

Martin Galloway is a culinary guru with over 20 years of experience in the food and hospitality industry.

A Certified Executive Chef for more than a decade, he has led the kitchens of Oglebay Resort, Savage River Lodge, and The Cornucopia Café, earning a reputation for excellence, creativity, and leadership.

In his capacity at MGT, Martin is not only the creativity behind the menu, but manages the events, provides Maple & Vine Market with his carry-out Chef-prepped meals, and is the Director of Culinary Operations.
